Services Provided by A Center For Grieving Children:

Support Groups:

A Center For Grieving Children provides a safe, caring environment which supports children and their families as they work through their grief and loss. There is no charge for attending our support groups.

Schools:

We are available to provide training and initiate support groups.

Crisis Intervention and Consultation:

We are available to schools, community and governmental agencies.

Education:

We provide workshops, presentations and in-service seminars to teachers, counselors, parents, individuals and community agencies on how children grieve and how to provide support to promote healing.

Community Outreach:

Representatives from A Center For Grieving Children go into the community to share information about the Center's programs and services.

Speaker Series:

We schedule speakers on a monthly basis who speak about family-related issues for our families and the public.

Library:

We have an extensive lending library that is available to the public.
