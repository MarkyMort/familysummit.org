Center Workshop

January 23 & 24, 2015

Supporting Children in Grief Workshop:

This workshop is for you if:

-You want to know more about how children grieve.

-You want to support children through the grief process.

-You desire to be more informed so that you may be able to use the training at home, in your profession, or at church.

(This training is a MUST for professionals who work with children!)

The Workshop Schedule:

Friday 5:45 - 9:00 p.m.

Saturday 9:00 a.m. - 12 noon

The training is $15 for those who wish to volunteer with our organization. For others who wish to attend our workshop, the cost is $55.

January 26, 2015

Additional Volunteer Training:

This training is required for individuals who are volunteering at our Center. It will be held the first Monday following the training from 6:00 - 8:00 p.m.

Workshops are held five (5) times during the year:

January, March, May, September, and November
