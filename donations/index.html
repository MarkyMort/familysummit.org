Your Support is Appreciated

Many of the children at A Center For Grieving Children have suffered multiple losses. In addition to the death of a loved one, some have had to move to a new location, change schools, and leave friends behind. Because A Center For Grieving Children is a non-profit, volunteer supported program, which is funded by the generosity of the community, including foundations, businesses, organizations, and individual donations, your support will help these children and their families move through the grief process. Although their lives will never be the same, through the loving support received at A Center for Grieving Children, the community, and yourself, they can begin to go on living and loving.

(A special thanks to: St. Benedict's Foundation supported by Mount Benedict Monastery, George & Dolores Eccles Foundation, and The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints Foundation.)

Although there is no cost for our services, donations are very much needed and greatly appreciated.
