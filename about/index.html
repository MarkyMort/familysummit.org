Principles and Mission

Our mission at A Center For Grieving Children is to provide a safe, caring environment where children, teens, and adults can share their feelings while they move through the healing process.

We believe that everyone should have the oppurtunity to grieve in a supportive and understanding environment and that:

  • Grief is a natural reaction to the loss of a loved one for children as well as adults.
  • Within each individual is the natural capacity to heal oneself.
  • The duration and intensity of grief are unique for each individual.
  • Caring and acceptance assist in the healing process.
